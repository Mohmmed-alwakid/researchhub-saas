## 📋 **OVERVIEW**

ResearchHub is a comprehensive user research platform that democratizes access to user insights through AI-powered automation, global participant networks, and real-time analytics. Our platform combines the simplicity of UserFeel, the enterprise scale of UserTesting, and the AI innovation of Maze.

## 🎯 **PLATFORM VISION**

### **Mission Statement**
Empower every product team to make data-driven decisions through accessible, AI-powered user research that delivers insights at the speed of product development.

### **Key Differentiators**
1. **🤖 AI-First Approach** - Automated interviews, intelligent analysis, smart recommendations
2. **🌍 Global Accessibility** - No geographic barriers, 40+ languages, diverse participant pool
3. **⚡ Speed to Insight** - Hours not weeks, real-time analysis, instant reports
4. **💡 Democratized Research** - No research experience required, guided workflows, template library
5. **🔐 Enterprise Ready** - SOC2, GDPR compliant, SSO, advanced security
