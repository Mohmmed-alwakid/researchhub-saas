📖 Manual Testing Instructions

  1. Start Local Development: npm run dev:fullstack
  2. Navigate to Study Builder: Go to /app/studies/new or edit an existing study
  3. Verify CollaborationHeader: Check that the collaboration header appears above the study form
  4. Test Sidebar Toggle: Click "Show Comments" or "Show Activity" to open the collaboration sidebar
  5. Verify Team Presence: Confirm that mock team members appear in the sidebar
  6. Test Real-time Updates: Verify that the study title in the collaboration header updates as you type
  7. Check Responsive Design: Ensure the sidebar doesn't interfere with the study building workflow
